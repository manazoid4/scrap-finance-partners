# Audience

## Primary audience

- Owners of UK scrap yards
- Directors of recycling businesses
- Regional waste/recycling operators
- Commercial directors
- Operations managers
- Transport managers
- Finance managers who need support
- Businesses using Fred, Xero, D365, Sage, spreadsheets or disconnected reporting
- Businesses with stock, transport, commercial and finance teams using different numbers

## Audience mindset

- Practical
- Busy
- Cost-conscious
- Suspicious of fluff
- Wants clear numbers
- Wants problems fixed
- Wants someone who understands the yard and the accounts
- Does not want generic consultancy language

## The reaction the site should produce

> This person understands our world.
