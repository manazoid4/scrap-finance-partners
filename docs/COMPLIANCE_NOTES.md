# Compliance Notes

Before trading, confirm:

- Legal business structure
- Professional accountancy body status
- AML supervision requirements
- Professional indemnity insurance
- Engagement letters
- GDPR/privacy policy
- Data processing agreement
- Client confidentiality process
- Tax/accountancy service boundaries
- Payroll service boundaries
- Whether Companies House authorised agent/ACSP status is needed
- What can be called consultancy vs accountancy vs tax vs audit vs regulated advice

## Accuracy rule

Do not claim Majid is chartered, licensed, regulated, an auditor, a tax adviser or a financial adviser unless confirmed later.

## Privacy rule

Do not expose personal phone number, email address or home details in public site copy unless explicitly approved. Use placeholders for contact information.

## Footer disclaimer (used site-wide)

> This website is for consultancy positioning and does not provide legal, tax, audit or regulated financial advice. Formal accountancy, tax, audit, AML or compliance services should be provided only through appropriately qualified and supervised professionals.
