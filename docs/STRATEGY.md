# Strategy — Scrap Finance Partners

## Brand name

**Scrap Finance Partners**

Why this name wins:

- Clear niche — says exactly who it's for and what it does.
- Instantly understandable to a scrap yard director in one glance.
- Strong search relevance for "scrap finance", "scrap accountant", "recycling finance support".
- Works as a consultancy name now, and stretches cleanly into a productised software brand later ("Scrap Finance OS").

## Alternative names considered (preserved, not used)

- Metal & Recycling Finance Partners
- Recycle Finance Solutions
- Circular Business Partners
- Waste & Recycling Business Partners
- Insight Business Partners
- Scrap Industry Finance
- GreenCycle Business Advisory

## Positioning

**Primary:** Finance clarity for scrap metal and recycling businesses.

**Supporting:** Practical outsourced finance intelligence for scrap yards, recyclers and circular businesses that need better reporting, cleaner systems and stronger commercial control.

## Ideal customer profile

- UK scrap metal / recycling / waste business, single site or multi-site.
- £2m-£50m turnover, owner-operator or small commercial board.
- Uses Fred, Xero, D365, Sage or a mix of spreadsheets and disconnected systems.
- Has stock, transport, commercial and finance functions that don't agree on the numbers.
- Cannot yet justify a full-time Finance Director or finance business partner.

## Core buyer problem

Many small and medium-sized scrap companies cannot justify hiring a full-time Finance Director or finance business partner, but they still need expert support for a few days each month.

## Core promise

We turn messy stock, transport, commercial and finance data into clear decisions.

## Pain points

- Stock records don't match physical reality.
- Margin is a guess, not a number.
- Transport cost per tonne is invisible.
- Cost centres are confusing, not useful.
- Month-end reporting takes too long and isn't trusted.
- PO and payroll processes are manual and error-prone.
- System migrations (Sage to D365, spreadsheets to Fred) create data integrity risk.

## Business model

1. **Entry** - one-off Finance Health Check (diagnostic, low commitment).
2. **Sprint** - fixed-scope project on one painful area (stock, Fred, transport, PO/payroll).
3. **Retainer** - Bronze / Silver / Gold monthly finance business partner support.
4. **Future product** - Scrap Finance OS, a reporting/insight portal.

## Differentiation

- 26 years *inside* scrap metal finance, not a generalist accountant learning the sector.
- Deep operational literacy: Fred, EWC codes, stock sheets, transport modules - not just ledgers.
- Proven, quantified impact (£120k saved, £20k and £90k overpayments recovered, 7-day to 2-day close).
- Speaks the language of the yard, not generic accountancy jargon.

## Founder advantage

Majid Ahmed has worked across Finance, Operations, Commercial, Transport and Systems inside real scrap and recycling businesses - not as an external adviser, but as the person who built the reports, fixed the stock sheets, and sat in the transport performance meetings. This is operator-grade finance knowledge, not consultancy theory.

## Future product direction

**Scrap Finance OS** - a future portal where clients upload reports and receive a KPI dashboard, margin analysis, stock discrepancy alerts, transport cost insight, a board pack, and action recommendations. Concept only at this stage - not built.
