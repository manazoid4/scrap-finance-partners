# Offer Ladder

## Entry offer

**Scrap Yard Finance Health Check**
Indicative price: £750-£1,500

A diagnostic engagement covering stock visibility, margin reporting, transport performance, cost-centre control, Fred/Xero/D365 data quality, PO workflow, payroll/commission process, and manual spreadsheet risk. Delivers a red/amber/green scorecard, root causes, quick wins and a 30-day plan.

## Sprint offers

Fixes stock reconciliation gaps, investigates discrepancies, rebuilds stock reporting rhythm.

**Fred / Reporting Improvement Sprint**
Indicative price: £1,500-£5,000
Improves Fred reports, stock sheets, EWC codes, new site setup, and system data integrity.

**Transport Finance Review**
Indicative price: £1,500-£4,000
Builds transport P&L visibility, cost-per-tonne reporting, fuel variance tracking, cost-centre control.

**PO, Payroll & Process Control Review**

Fixes PO approval workflow, payroll/commission accuracy, coding structure, manual process risk.

## Retainers

### Bronze - £500/month

Best for small operators needing monthly visibility.

Includes:
- Monthly KPI dashboard
- Stock position snapshot
- Basic management reporting
- Short commentary
- Improvement log

### Silver - £1,000/month

Best for growing operators needing monthly business-partner rhythm.

Includes everything in Bronze, plus:
- Forecasting
- Cashflow view
- Monthly business partner meeting
- Margin and stock movement analysis
- Transport/commercial KPI pack
- Action tracker

### Gold - £2,000+/month

Best for serious operators needing outsourced FD/business partner support.

Includes everything in Silver, plus:
- Board-style reporting pack
- Strategic finance support
- Cost-centre review
- Process improvement roadmap
- Systems/reporting oversight
- Regular decision support
