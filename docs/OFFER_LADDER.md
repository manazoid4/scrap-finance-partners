# Offer Ladder

## Pricing Principle

Price against recovered margin, stock accuracy, cash control and owner decisions, not against bookkeeping time. The buyer is not paying for reports; they are paying to find where money is leaking and to stop it happening again.

## Entry Offer

**Scrap Yard Finance Health Check**
Indicative price: £2,500 fixed

A one-week diagnostic covering stock visibility, margin reporting, transport performance, cost-centre control, Fred/Xero/D365 data quality, PO workflow, payroll/commission process, and manual spreadsheet risk.

Deliverables:
- Red/amber/green scorecard
- Recoverable-value estimate
- Root-cause findings
- 30-day owner action plan
- Recommended sprint or retainer path

## Sprint Offers

**Stock Accuracy Sprint**
Indicative price: £5,000-£12,000

Finds high-value stock discrepancies, traces variances to tickets, grades, suppliers or process gaps, and rebuilds the reporting rhythm.

**Fred / Reporting Improvement Sprint**
Indicative price: £4,000-£10,000

Improves Fred reports, stock sheets, EWC codes, site setup, transport reporting and export quality so the system produces usable management numbers.

**Transport Finance Review**
Indicative price: £5,000-£10,000

Builds transport P&L visibility, cost-per-tonne reporting, fuel variance tracking, cost-centre control and route/customer profitability.

**PO, Payroll & Process Control Review**
Indicative price: £4,000-£8,000

Fixes PO approval workflow, payroll/commission accuracy, coding structure and manual process risk.

**Outsourced FD Setup Month**
Indicative price: £8,000-£15,000

Builds the management pack, close rhythm, KPI model, meeting cadence and owner decision dashboard before the monthly retainer starts.

## Retainers

### Control - £1,500/month

Best for owner-managed yards that need the numbers trusted every month.

Includes:
- Monthly yard finance pack
- Stock position by grade
- Gross margin by material category
- Debtor days and cash watch
- Fred/Xero data quality log
- Monthly owner action list

### Margin - £3,000/month

Best for growing yards that need active margin, stock and transport control.

Includes everything in Control, plus:
- Margin per tonne by grade
- Transport cost per tonne
- LME versus buy-price review
- Cash flow forecast and debtor pressure list
- Monthly business partner meeting
- Loss-making grade and route flags
- Commercial decision tracker

### Board - £6,000+/month

Best for serious single-site or multi-site operators that want FD-level control without hiring one.

Includes everything in Margin, plus:
- Board-style management pack
- Quarterly bank/accountant pack
- Multi-site reporting structure
- Forecast, budget and scenario model
- KPI review and challenge meetings
- Payroll, commission and coding advisory
- System/process improvement roadmap
- Direct owner decision support
- Priority sprint access
