# Scrap Finance Partners — Venture Plan

## Working Positioning

A specialist UK consultancy for scrap metal, waste, recycling and circular economy businesses that need finance, reporting, stock visibility and commercial decision support without hiring a full-time Finance Director.

## Founder Advantage

- 26 years of accountancy and finance experience in UK scrap metal.
- Deep knowledge across Finance, Operations, Commercial, Transport and systems.
- Practical Fred system/reporting experience.
- Ability to translate messy stock, transport, product codes, EWC codes and management data into decisions.

## Best Name Direction

Primary recommendation: **Scrap Finance Partners**

Why: clear, niche, searchable, obvious to the buyer, and less generic than “Insight Business Partners”.

Alternative names:

1. **Scrap Industry Finance** — strongest keyword relevance.
2. **Circular Finance Partners** — more premium, broader recycling/waste positioning.
3. **Recycle Finance Solutions** — straightforward but slightly generic.
4. **GreenCycle Business Advisory** — more ESG/circular economy angle.
5. **Waste & Recycling Business Partners** — broadest, but long.

## Initial Offer Ladder

### Bronze — £500/month

For owner-managed scrap yards that need visibility.

- Monthly KPI dashboard.
- Stock position report.
- Basic management commentary.
- One monthly email summary.
- Simple improvement log.

### Silver — £1,000/month

For growing operators that need finance + commercial rhythm.

- Everything in Bronze.
- Forecasting and cashflow view.
- Monthly business partner meeting.
- Margin and stock movement analysis.
- Transport/commercial KPI pack.
- Action tracker.

### Gold — £2,000+/month

For companies that need an outsourced Finance Director/Business Partner.

- Everything in Silver.
- Strategic support.
- Board-style pack.
- KPI review meetings.
- System/process improvement roadmap.
- Payroll, commission and coding advisory.
- Owner decision support.

## High-Value Wedge Offers

These should be one-off diagnostic products that lead into retainers.

1. **Scrap Yard Finance Health Check** — £750–£1,500
   - Review reporting, stock, margin, processes and systems.
   - Deliver a red/amber/green improvement report.

2. **Stock Accuracy Sprint** — £1,500–£3,000
   - Investigate stock discrepancies and reporting weaknesses.
   - Build a corrected stock visibility process.

3. **Fred Reporting Improvement Sprint** — £1,500–£5,000
   - Improve Fred reports, stock sheets, EWC codes, new site setup and transport reporting.

4. **Outsourced FD Setup Month** — £2,500–£5,000
   - Build dashboard, reporting cadence, monthly management pack and meeting rhythm.
